For years I have wanted to sync my Christmas lights to music. &nbsp;Each year&nbsp;comes and goes without&nbsp;even a single blinking light on my house. &nbsp;But no more... &nbsp;this year I decided&nbsp;to finally&nbsp;sync my house to the radio station&nbsp;FM&nbsp;100.3!

Last night we turned it on for our first end-to-end test run. &nbsp;And here are the results. &nbsp;What do you think?

[youtube=http://youtu.be/8mJcbYkdWeM]

For those who are a little more curious about how it works, I'll be writing a much more detailed set of posts about it later. &nbsp;But a quick view of the pipeline is as follows:

Radio -\> Custom Audio Cable -\> Android -\> Frequency Magic -\> Bluetooth -\> Arduino -\> 16 Channel Relay -\>&nbsp;Christmas Lights

[youtube=http://youtu.be/VliNajNws5c]

And there's more to come. &nbsp;A few strands of lights&nbsp;are still in transit. &nbsp;And there's a variety of tweaks I&nbsp;still plan to make.
